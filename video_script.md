# Spotter ELD - Video Submission Script

## Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:15)
**(Visual: Title slide showing "Spotter ELD - Trip Planner & Log Generator" and the Spotter logo, then transitioning to the app's welcome screen.)**
**Speaker:** "Hi, I'm excited to present Spotter, an FMCSA-compliant ELD Trip Planner and Log Generator. For truck drivers, planning a route while strictly adhering to Hours of Service, or HOS, regulations can be complex and time-consuming. Spotter automates this entirely."

## Scene 2: Problem & Solution (0:15 - 0:30)
**(Visual: Split screen showing a manual paper logbook on one side, and the Spotter UI on the other.)**
**Speaker:** "Instead of manually calculating 11-hour driving limits, 14-hour on-duty windows, and mandated 30-minute breaks, Spotter takes your route details and automatically generates both the optimal path and compliant electronic log sheets."

## Scene 3: Inputting Trip Details (0:30 - 0:45)
**(Visual: Screen recording of the Spotter web app. The cursor moves to the input form on the left sidebar.)**
**Speaker:** "Let's plan a trip. In the sidebar, we enter our Current Location, Pickup Location, and Dropoff Location. We can also specify how many cycle hours we've already used. Once we hit 'Plan Trip', Spotter communicates with the backend to analyze distances, driving times, and HOS rules."

## Scene 4: Route Map (0:45 - 1:00)
**(Visual: Screen recording showing the map tab with the generated route, waypoints, and summary statistics on the sidebar.)**
**Speaker:** "The app instantly generates an interactive map of our route. On the left, we can see the Trip Summary: total distance, total duration, and a breakdown of the trip into distinct driving segments."

## Scene 5: ELD Logs Generation (1:00 - 1:30)
**(Visual: Screen recording switching to the 'ELD Logs' tab. Clicking through the 'Day 1', 'Day 2' pills to show the generated grid logs.)**
**Speaker:** "Now for the most powerful feature—switching to the ELD Logs tab, Spotter has generated complete, FMCSA-compliant Daily Log Sheets. It automatically calculates and graphs our off-duty time, driving time, on-duty time, and required breaks onto the standard 24-hour grid. We can toggle between days to see exactly when to drive and when to rest."

## Scene 6: Conclusion (1:30 - 1:45)
**(Visual: Screen recording zooming out to show the full app layout, then fading to a "Thank You" screen with contact info/name.)**
**Speaker:** "Spotter provides a modern, seamless full-stack solution to ensure route efficiency and legal compliance for the trucking industry. Thank you for watching!"
